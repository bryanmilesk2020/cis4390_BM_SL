### Who will use this dashboard?

#### 1) Small business owners

Permissions: View business data, write/store business data in ledger, change settings, edit users, modify permissions

#### 2) Auditors

Permissions: View business data, compare hashes to ensure data integrity

#### 3) Managers

Permissions: View business data

### Common use-case scenarios

#### Small business owners:

View revenue summaries, monitor expenses, check integrity status, monitor audits

#### Auditors

Verify records haven't been altered, investigate discrepancies, review historical changes

#### Managers

Monitor daily business activity, track operational changes, review inventory and sales patterns
